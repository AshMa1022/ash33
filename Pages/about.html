About Me

Ash Ma{馬文晴} is a graphic designer/painter/coder based in Providence, RI. They are pursuing a BFA in graphic design at the Rhode Island School of Design.

This handmade website is type set in Elm Mono design by myself ;3

Ash love doodling, staring into space, and sleeping in the rain.

Ash believe in the power of graphic design. By merging forms and color with daily experiences, they hope to bridge the story of theirs and yours.
