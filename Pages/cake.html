Birthdaycake

Code[OpenGL],Blender,Drawing / 2023

Realtime_filter(Birthdaycake) is Ash’s final project for CS1230 introduction to computer graphics.


This project implements a cake scene(object) with phong lightning, cel shading, three post-processing filters, and a semi-particle system of falling ribbon fragments.
