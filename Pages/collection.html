Collection of Knots

Poster / 2023

The Collection of Knots is a poster series organizing 50 knots in the category of [Knots that are Knots] and “Knots that are not Knots].

It later extended to a mini-exhibition that allowed people to knot their own collection, with corresponding instruction of tiding the knot.
