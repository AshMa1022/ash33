Cyborg Manifesto

for Design Studio I

Editorial, Book / 2022


This is a book re-design project of “A Cyborg Manifesto: science, technology, and socialist-feminism in the late twentieth century” written by Donna Haraway.

I wish to address one of the controversies in this essay during the design process which is its accessibility to its content— requiring the reader to have a lot of pre-knowledge of western ideology & continental philosophy. Therefore I use different forms of annotation to provide complementary information to these contents. There are punch holes on the right side that allows more flexibility in re-editing the book.
